# FUTURE_CS_02
🔍 Case Studies
1️⃣ Fake Bank Alert (FNB Phishing Email)

Subject: Urgent: Unusual Activity Detected on Your Account
Sender: support@fnb-secure-alerts.com

🚨 Key Findings:
Fake domain (not the official bank website)
Urgent threat (“account suspension in 24 hours”)
Generic greeting (“Dear Customer”)
⚠️ Risk Level: High (Phishing)
🧠 How the Attack Works:

The attacker pretends to be a bank and creates panic to force the victim into clicking a malicious link and entering login details.

2️⃣ Fake Job Offer Scam

Subject: Job Opportunity: Immediate Hiring
Sender: hr@amazon-careers-job.net

🚨 Key Findings:
Requests sensitive information (ID & banking details)
Suspicious domain name
No formal hiring process
⚠️ Risk Level: High (Phishing)
🧠 How the Attack Works:

The attacker offers a fake job opportunity to trick victims into sharing personal and financial information.

🛡️ Prevention Tips
✅ Do:
Verify sender email addresses carefully
Visit official websites directly (do not click links)
Report suspicious emails to IT/security teams
Confirm job offers through official company platforms
❌ Don’t:
Click on unknown or suspicious links
Share sensitive information via email
Trust urgent or threatening messages
Respond to unsolicited job offers
🔐 Key Takeaways
Always question urgency in emails
Check domains carefully (small changes can indicate scams)
Attackers rely on social engineering, not just technology
Awareness is the first line of defense
